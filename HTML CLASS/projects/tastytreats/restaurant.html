Texas Road House

This is one of my favorite restaurants. I do not go that often because it is a bit expensive for me. But they do really! Tasty food and rolls and honey butter are out of this world. I treat myself abet since I do not go there often so I sometimes get me a adult drink. If you have not ever been there, I recommend giving them a shot.
